Features

Profile

Accept new connection requests or star a member to easily follow them. Create public albums for anyone to see, or private albums to be shared with those you choose.

Personalize your experience with color themes that are visible to other users. Attach metatags to your profile to help others discover you in the People section.

Discussions

One of the core features of B • OUT are Discussions. This is where a lot of the action takes place. Our content contributors start original, thought-provoking Discussions on whatever topics they are writing about, and open them up to members.

Any members can also start their own Discussions and give their voice to the LGBTQ community. As with any community, there are some basic rules of the road, which you can find here.

Messages

Chat with other members within the B • OUT ecosystem. Send photos, videos, GIFs, as well as your current location within a conversation.

A unique feature of our messaging tool is that members can use the Media Filter to have complete control over what shows up on your screen, and what doesn’t.

Status

Share what's on your mind with others by posting a short Status update. Filter your Status updates by any location you choose, near or far.

And we've eliminated clutter and that FOMO that keeps you scrolling forever. Updating your Status replaces the one before.

People

Discover and meet new friends, network with LGBTQ professionals, or find someone to date. Easily let others know if you’re looking for casual intimacy -- or not -- with the Looking toggle.

Traveling? Instantly let other members know with our Travel toggle. Also filter members by gender, age, or fine-tune your search with meta-tags to find exactly who and what you're looking for.

Plans

Headed to the beach? Going to karaoke? Create a Plan for just you and your friends, or make your Plan public to let everyone know.

Plans are set with a start and end date and time. When your plan concludes, the post is automatically deleted. See what others are planning and doing. Know where to go and what to do.

Navigation

We have reimagined app navigation to make finding what you need as easy as possible.  See previews of each section on the homescreen to get an overview of what’s going on. And no more wasting time endlessly scrolling to see what you want. Use our collapsible pills to quickly get to the content you want.

With so much to do on B • OUT, you need an easy way to get home. Simply hold down anywhere on the screen to get back to the homescreen.

Ergonomics

An app designed from the bottom up for today's screen sizes. Use B • OUT entirely one-handed while on the go with our side capsules that change position based on your hand dominance.

Pull up on the menu bar to reveal the TouchPad remote, which allows you to easily navigate between the top banner cards.
